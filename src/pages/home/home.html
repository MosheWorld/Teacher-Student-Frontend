StudyHub
Built for teachers and students
StudyHub is a study platform inspired by the way you work. You can find teachers from your home and talk with them directly, and manage your teachers, you can also teach other people, keep it simple and fun.
StudyHub for individuals As individual user at StudyHub you can find teachers around Israel, talk with them directly at WhatsApp, rank teachers and tell your opinion about them.
