StudyHub
For students and private teachers
StudyHub is a study platform inspired by the way you work. You can find teachers from your home and talk with them directly, Manage your teachers & Teach other people.
Keep it simple and fun
StudyHub for individuals As individual user at StudyHub you can find teachers around Israel, talk with them directly at WhatsApp, rank teachers and tell your opinion about them. StudyHub for teachers As a teacher at StudyHub you'll be shown at students searches over the app, you will have your own profile and you will be able to manage it as you wish.
